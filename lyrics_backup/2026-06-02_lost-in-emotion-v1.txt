[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I feel the fire when our shadows intertwine
Like dusty roads that leave the empty miles behind
You pull me closer with a gentle, slow embrace
I find my haven in the warmth of this sweet space

[Verse 1]
The midnight moon is painting silver on your skin
Like porch light memories where all our dreams begin
We share a secret in the silence of the room
A steady fire burning through the heavy gloom

[Pre-Chorus]
Every breath is pulling me a little deeper
Every glance is telling me to be your keeper
There is a magic in the way we softly sway
I want to freeze this very moment, make it stay

[Chorus]
Lost in emotion, I am drowning in your eyes
An open highway where my deepest passion lies
I feel the rhythm of a slowly beating heart
I never want this heavy feeling to depart

[Post-Chorus]
[Echo] (Lost in emotion) I am never letting go
[Echo] (Lost in emotion) Letting all my feelings show

[Verse 2]
The morning sun is still a million miles away
I want to memorize the words you do not say
Your fingertips are tracing rivers on my chest
Like hometown borders where my wandering spirit rests

[Bridge]
The hands of time have stopped outside this quiet door
I have been longing and I cannot wait no more
A burning fever that is melting down my pride
I want to keep you right forever by my side
Just close your eyes and let the worry slowly fade
And let Saeed protect the promises we made

[Spoken]
I want to ride this feeling until the end of the line
Just you, just me, lost in the dark together
